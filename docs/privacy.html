Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 4, 2025

Privacy First: DailyQuipAI is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

1. Information We Don't Collect

DailyQuipAI does NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device information
  • Contact lists or photos
  • Any other personal data

2. Local Data Storage

All your learning progress and preferences are stored locally on your device only:

  • Daily learning progress
  • Saved knowledge cards
  • Category preferences
  • Learning goals and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

3. Third-Party Services

Content Generation: We use Google Gemini API to generate educational content. When generating cards:

  • Only category preferences are sent (e.g., "History", "Science")
  • No personal information is transmitted
  • Content requests are anonymous

In-App Purchases: Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles payment data.

4. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your data on our servers, there is no risk of data breaches from our end. All data remains securely on your device, protected by your device's security measures.

5. Children's Privacy

DailyQuipAI is designed to be safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing for us to delete, modify, or export. You maintain full control over your data by managing it directly on your device.
