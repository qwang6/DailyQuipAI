How does DailyQuipAI work?
DailyQuipAI uses AI to generate personalized knowledge cards based on your selected categories. Each day, you receive fresh, engaging content to expand your knowledge across topics like History, Science, Art, Technology, Philosophy, and Literature.
What's the difference between Free and Premium?
Free: 5 knowledge cards per day
Premium: Unlimited cards, customizable daily goals, and access to all features

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store:

1. Open Settings on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select DailyQuipAI
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

You'll continue to have Premium access until the end of your billing period.
Can I change my learning categories?
Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Preferred Categories to customize which topics you want to learn about.
How do I save cards for later?
Swipe right on any card to save it. You can review your saved cards anytime from the main menu.
Is my data private?
Absolutely! All your learning progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Read our Privacy Policy
What if I encounter a bug or issue?
Please email us at support@dailyquipai.app with details about the issue. Include your device model and iOS version if possible.
Can I use DailyQuipAI offline?
You need an internet connection to generate new cards. However, you can review previously loaded cards offline.
Do you offer refunds?

1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com

4. Select "Report a Problem" and follow the steps
